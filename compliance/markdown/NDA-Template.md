## Preamble

The Parties intend to collaborate on [Project Description]. During this collaboration, confidential information will be exchanged, which shall be protected by this Agreement.

## § 1 Confidential Information

Confidential Information includes all information that:
- Is marked as "confidential", "secret", or similar
- Is by its nature to be considered confidential
- Constitutes trade or business secrets
- Contains personal data
- Relates to technical data, system information, or security concepts

## § 2 Confidentiality Obligations

The Receiving Party agrees to:

1. Keep Confidential Information strictly secret
2. Use it only for the agreed purpose
3. Take appropriate security measures
4. Grant access only to authorized personnel
5. Not make copies without written permission

## § 3 Exceptions

The confidentiality obligation does not apply to information that:
- Is or becomes publicly known
- Was lawfully obtained from third parties
- Was already known
- Must be disclosed due to legal obligations

## § 4 Data Protection

When processing personal data, the provisions of GDPR must be observed. A separate Data Processing Agreement may be required.

## § 5 Return of Materials

Upon termination of the collaboration, all Confidential Information must be:
- Returned immediately
- Deleted/destroyed upon request
- Deletion/destruction must be confirmed in writing
